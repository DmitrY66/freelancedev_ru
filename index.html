Some of the works

A website can’t just look good, it needs to work well too. My development have the experience and expert technical skills to ensure all my sites achieve their objectives.

I’m primarily a front end developer. Which means for the most part I will be focusing on writing semantic HTML markup, PHP, JS, scalable and reusable CSS with SASS or LESS. All of my work is handcrafted. I build projects with , suitably for the current and future scope of any size.

Quality work requires an integrated approach. Order a call on the site using the form or just write me an e-mail. We will discuss all the details in detail, come up with the best solution and I will draw up a technical assignment. After agreeing on the terms of reference and 50% of the advance payment, I will proceed with the task.

  • Discussion of the problem, solution, look at the design, if any, and I say the cost of work and terms.
  • We agree on the terms of reference. If necessary, we conclude a contract, and I start work.
  • Layout, programming, customization of functionality, etc., according to the terms of reference.
  • Testing the finished product. I check for errors, performance, display in browsers or on mobile devices. Everything is in accordance with the terms of reference.
